I am fascinated by robots because they combine mechanics, electronics, and code into one system. Robots can help people explore dangerous places, assist in surgery, or simply make everyday tasks easier. In the future, robots will likely work alongside humans in more jobs and even learn from their environments.

Travel lets me experience new cultures, foods, and landscapes that are completely different from home. I enjoy walking through historic streets, visiting museums, and trying local dishes. Every trip teaches me something new and inspires me to keep exploring the world.
